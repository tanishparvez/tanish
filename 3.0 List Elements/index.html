Tanish's Biriyani Recipe

Ingredients:

For the dough:

  • 2 cups of basmati rice
  • 4 cups of water
  • 1 tablespoon of ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1 teaspoon of salt

For the filling:

  • 500g of chicken, cut into small pieces
  • 2 cups of yogurt
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons of biriyani masala
  • 1 teaspoon of turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon of red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon of coriander powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • 2 tablespoons of cooking oil

Instructions

  1. Rinse the basmati rice under cold water until the water runs clear.
  2. In a large pot, bring 4 cups of water to a boil.
  3. Add the rinsed rice, ghee, and salt to the pot.
  4. Stir well, cover, and cook over medium heat until the rice is tender and all the water is absorbed, about 15-20 minutes.
  5. Once cooked, remove from heat and let it cool.
  6. In a large bowl, marinate the chicken pieces with yogurt, biriyani masala, turmeric powder, red chili powder, cumin seeds, coriander powder, and salt. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  7. In a separate pan, heat cooking oil over medium heat. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  8. Add chopped tomatoes and green chilies to the pan. Cook until the tomatoes are soft and mushy.
  9. Add the marinated chicken mixture to the pan and cook until the chicken is tender and cooked through.
  10. Once the chicken is cooked, remove from heat and set aside.
  11. Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C).
  12. Grease a baking dish with ghee or cooking oil.
  13. Take half of the cooked rice and spread it evenly on the bottom of the baking dish.
  14. Spread the cooked chicken mixture on top of the rice layer.
  15. Cover the chicken layer with the remaining cooked rice.
  16. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  17. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the biriyani is heated through.
  18. Once done, remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
  19. Serve the delicious biriyani hot with raita (yogurt sauce) and enjoy the burst of flavors!
